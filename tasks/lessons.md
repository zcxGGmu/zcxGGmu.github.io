# Lessons

- 2026-06-09: When debugging generated static site styling, verify the deployed CSS cascade at the affected viewport before changing content or HTML.
- 2026-06-10: Article `featured_image` assets are used as hero backgrounds with overlaid post metadata, so they should be title-free or purely decorative; keep title-card artwork inside the article body instead.
- 2026-06-10: After changing a hero image, also inspect the first in-article image at the actual article column width; a full-size SVG can look acceptable while its text and diagram nodes become cramped when scaled down.
